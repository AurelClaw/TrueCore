**Agent architecture** in [computer science](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Computer_science "Computer science") is a [blueprint](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Blueprint "Blueprint") for [software agents](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Software_agent "Software agent") and [intelligent control](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Intelligent_control "Intelligent control") systems, depicting the arrangement of components. The architectures implemented by [intelligent agents](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Intelligent_agent "Intelligent agent") are referred to as [cognitive architectures](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cognitive_architecture "Cognitive architecture").[[1]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/agent_architecture#cite_note-1) The term agent is a conceptual idea, but not defined precisely. It consists of facts, set of goals and sometimes a plan library.[[2]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/agent_architecture#cite_note-SterlingTaveter2009-2)
